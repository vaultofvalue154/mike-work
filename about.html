About SRC Sports Academy

Discover our journey in promoting sporting excellence

Our Mission

To promote sports development and excellence within the university community and surrounding regions through accessible, high-quality training programs that inspire athletic achievement and personal growth.

Our Vision

To become a leading regional sports institution recognized for developing athletes of character, fostering community engagement, and advancing the sporting capabilities of our university and its locality.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Commitment to high standards in all sporting activities
  • Integrity: Honesty and ethical conduct in all interactions
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming athletes of all backgrounds and skill levels
  • Community: Building strong connections among athletes, coaches, and families
  • Growth: Continuous improvement and personal development

Funding & Support

The SRC Sports Academy is supported by:

  • University Student Representative Council (SRC) funding
  • Local sports bodies and athletic associations
  • Community partnerships and sponsorships
  • Dedicated coaching professionals

Our Sports Programs

We offer comprehensive training in both traditional and emerging sports:

  • Team Sports: Football, Rugby, Handball, Volleyball
  • Individual Sports: Table Tennis, Gymnastics, Ballet
  • Combat Sports: Wrestling, Taekwondo, Judo
