Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! Our programs are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. We have classes tailored to your experience.

What equipment is provided?

We provide most training equipment. Check with your sport's coordinator about specific requirements, and we'll provide a detailed list.

Are there age restrictions?

We offer programs for youth and adults. Age-appropriate training ensures safety and optimal learning. Contact us for age-specific details.

Can I try a trial class?

Absolutely! We offer trial sessions for new participants. Register on our booking page and specify your interest.
